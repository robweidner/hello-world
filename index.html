How It Works

You give the order. They run the play.

01

Dispatch

Tell the CEO Agent what you need. A landing page. A competitive analysis. A full product launch. One message kicks off the entire machine.

02

Coordinate

The Chief of Staff decomposes your request into discrete tasks, assigns each to the right specialist, and manages dependencies. Think air traffic control for AI.

03

Execute

Specialist agents work in parallel — the Developer ships code, Marketing drafts copy, Creative generates visuals, QA stress-tests everything.

04

Deliver

Results flow back through the chain — quality-checked, coordinated, and tracked in your command center. You review. They iterate. Ship faster than any human team.
